About Catering


Tradition since 1889

Welcome to Champaran Dhaba. We are a family-owned and operated restaurant located in the heart of saket, Delhi, serving authentic All Indian cuisine. Our restaurant is named after the district of Champaran in Bihar, India, which is known for its rich and flavorful cuisine. At Champaran Dhaba, we are passionate about providing our guests with an unforgettable dining experience. We use only the freshest and highest quality ingredients to create our dishes, and our menu features a variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options. In addition to our delicious food, we take great pride in our warm and welcoming atmosphere. Our restaurant is designed to transport you to the vibrant streets of India, with colorful decor and traditional Indian music playing in the background. Whether you are looking for a quick lunch, a romantic dinner for two, or a place to host your next event, Champaran Dhaba has something for everyone. Come visit us and experience the true flavors of North India.seasonal ingredients.
